Business Plan

“Flex inc. aims to seamlessly solve household problems using the flexbot in a quick and simple manner.”
What the statement means-

Core values - Our main goal is to be able to provide our services to people worldwide. At the moment, many people around the world overpay for easy fixes. We want our products to provide simple, easy and cheaper fixe. Our company is determined to make and keep improving our Flexbots.

How we promote our values - We promote our values by making our robot honest, reliable, and loyal.

Organizational Structure

Founder, co-founder, project manager, Design vice president, Software vice president, Marketing Vice President, Manufacturing Vice President Flex Inc. is lead by their Founder and Co-Founder. The Founder and Co-Founder work together with the four units:Design, Software, Marketing, Manufacturing. The four units meet every week at our headquarters. Then the four units cooperate to make the Flexbot. The Marketing makes the Flexbot sell out by advertising the Flexbot. While the Design, Software and Manufacturing improves the Flexbot by looking at what difficulty the customers are having.

SWOT Analysis

Strength: Flexbot fixes things quickly and is trustworthy. Weakness: Flexbot might crash under extreme conditions and might be more expensive than other fixing robots. Opportunity: We could maybe improve on the efficiency and durability of our robot. Threats: Other fixing robots might be more efficient than ours and already have experience in the market, which wouldn’t be good for us.
